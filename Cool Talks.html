Most Recent Episodes:

EP.28: Charly García

In this episode, we talked with Charly García from the wrestling channel "Desde la Tercera". We talked about the

present of pro-wrestling, how he started in the world of YouTube, the community that he has created with Alan J.

from SuperKickTV in social media, the role of Bad Bunny in this year's WrestleMania, and other things related to

the world of pro-wrestling and social media.

EP.27: Pala

In this episode, we talked with Pala, a singer from Culiacan, Sinaloa that's just starting in this hard world. He

creates urban lating music, which is Reggaeton and Trap. We talked about how he started in the world of musc, what

he wants to transmit with his music, the difficuty that urban music really has, etc.

EP.26: BREG Sport

In this episode, we talked with Brandon Espinoza, the host of the YouTube channel "BREG Sport". His channel is about

soccer and that's what this episode was about. We also talked about how he started creating videos and what are

his plans for the future, but in majority the podcast was about the world of soccer.

EP.25: Eduardo Guízar

In this episode, we talked with one of the greates scientists that Mexico has ever seen, Eduardo Guízar. A man from

Sinaloa that has work in robotichs for many years. He was a very important part of the Curiosity robot that's

exploring the surface of Mars, specifically he worked in the wheels of the robot. We talked about how's the work

environment in NASA and how's the aerospacial industry entering Sinaloa.

EP.24: Miguel Taniyama

In this episode, we talked with the biggest chef of Sinaloa, Miguel Taniyama, who has a variety of restaurants

around the city. His family comes from Japan, so one of the main topics of this episode was the influence of

Japanese cuisine in today's food, what's really behind being the owner of a restaurant and the experience he

had working in the biggest aguachile ever and teaching his cooking technics to young talents.
